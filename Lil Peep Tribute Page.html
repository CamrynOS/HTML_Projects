Lil Peep

The artist who changed millions of lives

A time line of Lil Peep's life:

  • November 1, 1996 - Born in Allentown, PA
  • 2012 - Began recording music in his bedroom
  • 2015 - Started to release music including the Feelz EP, Lil Peep Pt. 1, and Live Forever
  • 2016 - Joined GBC (Goth Boi Clique); Released Crybaby and Hellboy
  • 2017 - Released Come Over When You're Sober Pt. 1
  • November 15, 2017 - Died due to a fentanyl/xanax overdose

"Everything changes with time, you can't predict where you're gonna be next year you have no idea."

-- Lil Peep
